How it works

Goal: complete today’s image by dragging tiles from the Pieces Tray onto the Board in the right spots.

  • Drag & drop: Move a tile from the tray onto a board cell. To move it elsewhere, drag it back to the tray first.
  • Hint: Shows a brief overlay of the solved image (5s).
  • Reset: Restart the same daily puzzle.
  • Flash memory: If you remove a piece that was correct, that cell dims briefly to remind you it was correct before.
  • Daily reset: New puzzle unlocks at 06:00 UTC everyday.

Each day, the puzzle will feature the PFP of a core believer of Billions Network (either the team, moderator, community member, etc.)
